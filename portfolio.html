Anime Ranking Project

The Best Movies According to Me

I WANT TO EAR YOUR PANCREAS

Shiga Haruki : In "I Want to Eat Your Pancreas," Shiga Haruki is a compassionate, introverted high school student. With a chance discovery of a diary belonging to his terminally ill classmate, Sakura, their lives entwine unexpectedly. As the story unfolds, Haruki transforms from a reserved bookworm to a companion who cherishes each moment, guided by Sakura's zest for life. Their poignant connection explores themes of mortality, friendship, and the beauty of living fully. Haruki's journey through sorrow and growth encapsulates the emotional core of this touching narrative, leaving a lasting impact on readers' hearts.

Sakura Yamauchi : Sakura Yamauchi, a vibrant character from "I Want to Eat Your Pancreas," exudes life despite her terminal illness. Her serendipitous encounter with a classmate unravels a poignant journey of shared moments and hidden emotions. Sakura's unyielding spirit, zest for life, and the diary she leaves behind challenge perceptions of mortality. Through her, the story explores the beauty of living fully, even in the face of inevitable farewells.

A SILENT VOICE

Shōya Ishida : Shōya Ishida is a fictional character from the novel "A SILENT VOICE" Initially distant and aloof, he discovers a diary belonging to Sakura, a classmate with a terminal illness. Through her words, Shōya evolves, forming a poignant connection that changes his perspective on life and relationships.

Shōko Nishimiya : Shōko Nishimiya, in "A SILENT VOICE" is a gentle and deaf girl. Her arrival sparks an unexpected friendship with the protagonist. Through her diary, they connect deeply, sharing life's joys and struggles. Shōko's presence compels exploration of empathy, acceptance, and the fleeting beauty of existence.

DARLING IN THE FRANXX

Hiro : In "Darling in the Franxx," Hiro (016) is the protagonist, initially labeled a "parasite." His determination and connection with Zero Two lead to his growth as a skilled Franxx pilot. Overcoming challenges, Hiro's evolution symbolizes both personal development and the show's themes of identity and relationships.

Zero-Two : Zero-Two, a central character in "Darling in the Franxx," is a enigmatic and alluring half-human, half-Klaxosaur pilot. With striking pink hair, red horns, and a rebellious demeanor, she captivates audiences. Her insatiable thirst for human emotions and quest for identity drive the anime's narrative, as she forms a unique bond with Hiro, the protagonist.
